    How to read this: Two numbers, two jobs. Revenue shows the size of the gap (or surplus) between your current conversion rate and a healthy 3% benchmark, it only moves when you change traffic, conversion rate, or order value. Profit takes that same gap and shows what it's actually worth in your pocket after costs, so it also moves when you adjust the margin slider on its own. If you only touch the margin slider, expect just the Profit number to move, that's correct, not a bug: margin doesn't change how many orders you're missing, only what each missed order was actually worth.
